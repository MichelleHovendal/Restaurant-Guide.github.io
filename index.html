About this webpage

This webpage is made by Ellen Winther Mortensen, Julius Pulvertaft Rasmussen, and Michelle Siw Hovendal and is part of the final project in the course Social Data Analysis and Visualization at Technical University of Denmark (DTU). This webpage is made in spring 2021.

The aim of the webpage is about restaurants placed in North America, the data is based on yelp data. The Python explainer notebook is attached here.

An introduction video has been made to explain and get a feeling of what the webpage can do. Please enjoy.
